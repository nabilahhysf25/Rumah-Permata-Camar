Activities & Programs

Our activities focus on education, character development, religious guidance, and community involvement.

Holistic Development

At Rumah Permata Camar, children are nurtured through structured daily activities designed to develop their academic abilities, moral values, discipline, and social skills. These programmes aim to prepare them for a responsible and independent future.

Activity Categories

Education | Religious & Moral | Community & Social

Educational Activities

  • ● Formal schooling and academic monitoring
  • ● Tuition and homework guidance
  • ● Examination preparation and motivation sessions

Religious & Moral Development

  • ● Daily prayers and Quran recitation
  • ● Islamic classes and moral guidance
  • ● Character building and discipline programmes

Community & Social Activities

  • ● Community service and charity involvement
  • ● Sports, recreation, and outdoor activities
  • ● Festive celebrations and special events
