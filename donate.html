Your contribution helps us provide shelter, education, and a better future for underprivileged children.

Why Your Support Matters

Rumah Permata Camar relies on public donations to sustain daily operations including food, education, utilities, healthcare, and general welfare of the children.

The estimated monthly operating cost is approximately RM30,000.
